## Design Context

Strategic design context lives in [`PRODUCT.md`](PRODUCT.md) (register, users, brand personality, anti-references, design principles) and the visual system in [`DESIGN.md`](DESIGN.md) (palette, typography, components). Read both before changing UI. Register is `product`; the guiding feel is calm and tidy, every action reversible.
